About Dragontail

Dragontail.co.uk was created as a project to test the capabilities of the original Raspberry Pi 1 model B+ by connecting it to a Maplin Weather Station in 2012. The original model 1 B+ is still running and processing the weather data.

Good luck and may the weather be with you.
